Faith

Faith is an important topic to me as I’m a Christian. I was raised in a protestant household. Even though I was raised in a religious household I've always questioned since I was a kid: wether God existed, if yes, is He good? Why would there be a Hell? Why are we alive in this World? Are here to suffer and go through challenges just to prove ourselves worth? What is it all?.

Nowadays, after being agnostic for many years, through my path I found reasons to have faith. I'm constantly seeking to deepen my understanding of God. I know I’m imperfect, often wrestling with personal challenges, yet my faith gives me hope to grow and become more Christ-like over time.
